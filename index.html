About

The Story Behind
Blacksea Growth Partners

Blacksea Growth Partners is a personal investment portfolio founded on the belief that capital and purpose are not mutually exclusive. Built by a Filipino IT professional with over a decade of experience in information systems development.

The portfolio spans three continents — Philippine, US, and European markets — with a focused thesis on the industries shaping the next decade: artificial intelligence, healthcare, clean energy, and sustainable real estate.

Every holding is intentionally selected to align with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, ensuring that financial returns contribute to a broader vision of global progress by 2030 and beyond.
